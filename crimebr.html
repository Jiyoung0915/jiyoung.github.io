Night-time thieves in Baton Rouge in 2018


There is a research from The Harris Poll that around 60 million Americans have been affected by identity theft. Theft incidents are one of the highest crime types in Baton Rouge.
Thus, we are visualized the theft incidents as a GIF (Graphics Interchange Format) image. We also visualized the most common types of crime in census block group.
For the 'Top Crime' layer, we have four types of crimes: Theft , Battery, Vehicle burglary, and Narcotic.
