Baton Rouge


There are four types of layers about East Baton Rouge Parish, Louisiana.
We can choose basemaps and layers. Let's explore these datasets!

For the 'Home-ownership' layers, there are home-ownership and home-rent.
For the 'Vacancy rate' layers, there are High vacancy rate , Average vacancy rate , and Low vncancy rate .
For the 'Crime rate' layers, the crime rate is calculated by dividing the number of reported crimes by the total population;
the result is multiplied by 100,000. The higher crime rate shows the darker red color.

Data resource: census block group , Drug and gun free zone from the East Baton Rouge GIS (EBRGIS),
and crime incidents from Open Baton Rouge.
